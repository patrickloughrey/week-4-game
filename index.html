Balloon Collector

At the start of the game you will be given a random number.

There are four balloons below. By clicking on any of balloons, you will increase your points by a random number, contributing to your total score.
Your objective is to match your total score to the random number given at the start of the game. You lose if your total score is more than the random target number.
The value of each balloon is completely hidden to you, so you will have to do calculations along the way.

At the start of each game, the balloons will have different values.
